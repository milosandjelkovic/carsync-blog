Multiple Turo accounts

How many times have you logged out of Turo app, only to log in again with different credentials? If for any reason you manage multiple accounts, the answer is probably a lot! CarSync comes to the rescue!

We are happy to announce that CarSync now supports more than one account per platform!

You are now able to connect more than one Turo/Getaround/Hyrecar/Ryde account and have access to reservations feed (for all accounts), revenue dashboard and live map with GPS location from a single interface.

This feature is perfect for:

  • Family members who are all into car sharing. #turolife
  • You have Turo accounts under different LLCs.
  • You’re a host who manages other people’s fleets.

We won’t stop there! We plan to work on more features to support multi-user and multi-account management. For example, do you really want to share your Turo credentials with your employees? No, you want to add them as separate users with limited privileges — if you could.

CarSync will allow just that. 24h post-trip check? Yes. Message renters? Yes. See financials? No! You will be able to choose what your employees can and can’t do. CarSync will be the keys to your car sharing business.

Stay tuned and give it a try at https://carsync.io — it’s FREE.

NOTE: CarSync is not affiliated with Turo in any way. We simply make it easy for hosts to take advantage of multiple platforms!
